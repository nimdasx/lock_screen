# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-04-27

Thank you for choosing to use **Quick Lock** ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. 

## 1. Information We Collect
We **do not** collect, store, share, or transmit any of your personal information, usage data, or analytics. The App operates completely offline and all processes happen locally on your device.

## 2. Permissions
To provide its core functionality, the App may request specific system permissions (such as Device Administrator, Accessibility Services, or similar screen-locking capabilities). These permissions are strictly used **only** to allow the App to lock your screen or manage lock screen features. We do not use these permissions to access, monitor, or retrieve any of your personal data or device usage history.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The App does not use any third-party services, trackers, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

## 4. Children's Privacy
Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact the developer at **nimdasx@gmail.com**.
