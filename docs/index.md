

# Quick Lock

Welcome to the official page for **Quick Lock**, a simple, fast, and secure screen lock application for Android.

## Features
- **One-Tap Lock**: Quickly lock your screen without using the physical power button.
- **Privacy First**: We do not collect or share any of your personal data. The app works entirely offline.
- **Lightweight**: Optimized for performance and battery life.
